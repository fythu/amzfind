About Us | Amazon Seller Assistant

Welcome to Amazon Seller Assistant, an efficient operational toolset tailored specifically for cross-border e-commerce sellers, especially Amazon sellers. Searching for advantageous supply sources, comparing prices across platforms, and managing product libraries efficiently are daily challenges for sellers. Our goal is to provide a one-stop intelligent solution to comprehensively improve your operational efficiency.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026 Latest Revision

Protecting user business data security and personal privacy is the highest principle for Amazon Seller Assistant. We fully implement minimized data collection and decentralized local storage.

1. How We Collect Data

  • Local Business Data: All tracked info including ASINs, prices, and wishlists are strictly forced to be saved into your browser's local storage. Our cloud servers do not save your private selections.
  • Network Request Logs: For external connections, requests are sent temporarily and instantly discarded. None are retained.

2. 3rd Party Compliance

We promise never to sell, rent or share your data to untrained advertising intermediaries.
